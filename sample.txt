Microsoft Cloud Solution Architect - Ramp-Up Guide

Key Requirements:
1. Complete Azure Fundamentals certification within 30 days
2. Shadow 3 customer engagements in the first 60 days
3. Deliver your first technical presentation by day 90

Important Dates:
- Day 30: AZ-900 certification deadline
- Day 60: First customer meeting debrief due
- Day 90: Ramp-up review with manager

The main subject of this document is ramp-up guidance for new Cloud Solution Architect hires at Microsoft.
